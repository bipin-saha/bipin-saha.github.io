From 2D Perception to BEV Fusion: Implementation Roadmap

A staged roadmap for teams moving from single-camera models to multi-sensor bird's-eye-view fusion systems.

Problem

Many teams have strong 2D detectors but no structured migration plan to BEV-centric perception.

Method

Progress through calibration hardening, synchronized data pipelines, BEV supervision design, and iterative ablations.

Results

A staged migration minimizes regression risk and improves spatial consistency for downstream planning modules.
