NetraJal : A Gaze Controlled Bangla Keyboard for Physically Challenged People

SparkerS

Netrajal is multipurpose software for disabled people to help them in their daily works. It is specially designed for paralyzed people who cannot move or move hardly. A paralyzed person usually cannot move their other body parts but can move their eyeball. Our software takes that movement as an input signal and does the required operation. The process is done by gaze detection, and thus we are using the built-in laptop camera for the detection purpose. Netrajal can do a wide range of operations, including internet surfing, Research, Business purpose, and there is also an option for emergency calls. Moreover, Netrajal can be operated in Bengali, too, which is a unique feature for this field. In a word, Netrajal is a complete package for disabled people to do things using computers. View

UAV : Intelligent Monitoring

Gravity Destroyer

Gravity Destroyer is a drone made by team Sparkers. The drone is a quadcopter having 4 rotors along with propellers. The drone is a usual drone used around the world, it can fly it can land, and it can take photos and videos, but why then we are saying it is a project! Of course, there are some unique features of the drone. Firstly the drone is super stable in the air. It can fly and stand still in midair without moving! We know, stability is one of the significant issues with drones as it operates in a three-dimensional area! As it is stable, it can take stable photos and shoot videos. Again we tried to make the drone at a little cost. We had made it by spending 30 Thousand Taka (356$) only. The drone has 4 brushless outrunner motors, the ESCs are 20A and provides excellent support. We have also included Compass, humidity, Free Falling, Autopilot sensors in the drone for getting various kinds of data. So, it can be used for weather forecast purposes too. We can operate the drone through radio control. We have used a medium-range transmitter and receiver to control it. It also has Telemetry support to control it from anywhere in the world with an internet connection. The coding part is also interesting as we have used the telemetry sensor, which allows us to code wirelessly. The brain of the drone is Ardupilot 2.8 Pro, which is a potent tool for these purposes. Again the power consumption of the drone is relatively low, so we get more fly time. The drone flies almost 20-25 minutes, and we have used a 27C Lithium Polymer battery to power it up. The powerful motors can lift the drone up to 10 km Omnidirectional, and the drone can be operated within a 2km meter radius. We had tested it several times, and Gravity Destroyer didn’t disappoint us! The drone would also provide the images of the area, which can be very handy too! So, this is the basic outline of our drone Gravity Destroyer and our future plan about the drone. View

Landmine Detecting Robot

SparkerS

Team Sparkers had developed a Landmine detecting BoT, which could detect landmines under the ground. We know, mines are so destructive, and the havoc id also so much. In the second world war and at various times, landmines have been used to destroy enemy tanks, soldiers, and equipment. But the problem is often they had left landmines which did not blast. As a result, the areas became much dangerous, and many civilian and military personal had died and have been dying by stepping in the minefields. There are no possible options to defuse a landmine, the only way out is to blast it. So soldiers have to find them through manual searching through a metal detector, and as it is not safe, accidents keep happening, and people die. To save those lives, we had created our BoT. The robot is a 4 wheeler car that can move to difficult surfaces and can move automatically by avoiding obstacles through its Sonar sensor. It also contains a moving metal detector that can detect landmines, and the detector can move around 180 degrees. The BoT can operate itself, but if we want to control it manually and search for mines, we can also do it by our smartphones. Again, the BoT detects any mine, it sends the signal to the Base station and also provides a loud sound that confirms the existence of the landmine. The prototype of us was made by keeping in mind the safety of the mass people living in landmine areas. We had used the Arduino UNO microcontroller to operate the BoT. Again Keeping in mind that the BoT could fall victim to the landmines too, we had kept the weight of the BoT down to the safety level. For further security, the BoT would send continuous data to the base station before it gets destroyed, so even if we lose our BoT, we will have some valuable data that can be used in the future for further work. That is the basic outline of our Landmine Detecting BoT. View
